Plus Installation with a Google Account

note

Please refer to the page at the link below for alternative installation methods for UModeler X Plus.

  • Sign up with an email address and install
  • For existing UModeler X Beta/Basic users

How to Install UModeler X Plus​

1. Purchase the UModeler X Plus version from the Unity Asset Store.​

2. Open your Unity project.​

3. Open the Unity Editor and go to Window > Package Manager from the top menu. Download and import the UModeler Hub from the UModeler X Plus package.​

4. After downloading, click the UModeler Hub icon that appears at the top center of the Scene to open the window.​

5. Click Create a New Account at the bottom.​

6. Click the Sign up with Google button, agree to the terms, and click Sign up with Google ID.​

7. On the "Unlock your UModeler X Plus" page, enter the Order ID or Invoice number for your purchased Plus version, then click the Unlock button.​

[How to find your Invoice number]

8. The Install button for UModeler X Plus will be activated. Click it to proceed with the installation.​
